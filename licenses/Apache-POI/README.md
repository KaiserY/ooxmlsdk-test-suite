the corpus imported under `corpus/Apache-POI`.

upstream licensing and notice terms.

`913c78891bd0cd20945b050c63abfb8c66c88009`. The checked-in `LICENSE` and
`NOTICE` files match `legal/LICENSE` and `legal/NOTICE` at that commit.
